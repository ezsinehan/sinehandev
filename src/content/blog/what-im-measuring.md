date: "2025-12-10"
summary: "Success metrics for the first version: clarity, speed, and a maintainable publishing loop."
tags: ["strategy", "performance"]
---

I am not optimizing for volume yet.

I am optimizing for consistency:

- can I publish without context switching too much?
- does each post remain easy to read on laptop and mobile?
- does the page still feel light and fast?

If those stay true, this can scale naturally.
