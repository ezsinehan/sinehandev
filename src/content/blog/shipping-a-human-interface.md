I wanted the new blog to feel like it belongs to the same room as the rest of the site: handwritten, quiet, and a little playful.

The hardest part was not styling a card. The hard part was choosing constraints and honoring them repeatedly:

- keep lines readable
- keep interactions calm
- keep maintenance low enough that publishing still feels fun

This first pass does not try to be fancy. It tries to be honest.
