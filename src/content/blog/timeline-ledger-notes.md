A timeline has one superpower: scanability.

You can glance at the left rail, anchor yourself in time, then read only what matters. For a small personal blog that will grow gradually, that signal is more useful than dense card mosaics.

I still wanted warmth, so each entry keeps a paper-like surface and tag chips. The structure is strict; the tone stays soft.
